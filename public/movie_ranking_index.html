My top 3 movies of all-time

John Wick: Chapter 4


With the price on his head ever increasing, John Wick uncovers a path to defeating The High Table. But before he can earn his freedom, Wick must face off against a new enemy with powerful alliances across the globe and forces that turn old friends into foes.

Extraction 2


Tasked with extracting a family who is at the mercy of a Georgian gangster, Tyler Rake infiltrates one of the world's deadliest prisons in order to save them. But when the extraction gets hot, and the gangster dies in the heat of battle, his equally ruthless brother tracks down Rake and his team to Sydney, in order to get revenge.

Everybody Hates Chris

Everyone Hates Chris is while growing up at Bedford-Stuyvesant, Brooklyn, New York City, an American television storyline sit-com that depicts the troubled experiences of comedian Chris Rock.
The show's title parodies the hit CBS sitcom Everybody Loves Raymond, in which Rock stated: "Everybody Loves Raymond, however Everyone Hates Chris!" .
